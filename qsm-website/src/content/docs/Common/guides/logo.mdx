While I am not a logo designer, I am trying my best.

There are 3 variants of the logo, small, medium, and large. The large variant
is rarely used, but it is the first iteration of the logo.

The small logo is used on the icon of this site, while the medium is used on 
the hero of the home page, and the large logo is used... nowhere besides this page.

<figure>
    <Image src={QSMMediumMark} alt="QSM medium logo with wordmark" height="200" style="background: white;" />
    <figcaption>The medium logo with a wordmark</figcaption>
</figure>

The medium logo is the only one with the wordmark variant, specifically used 
for the navigation bar of this site.

The logo and wordmark makes use of my favorite font, [Inter](https://rsms.me/inter/)
(specifically version 4.1)

The logos can be downloaded in various variants and formats from 
https://github.com/lines-of-codes/QSMSharp/tree/master/icon

The logo is created in Inkscape. File names ended with `_inkscape.svg` is an 
Inkscape SVG file.
